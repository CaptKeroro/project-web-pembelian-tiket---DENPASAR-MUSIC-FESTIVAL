About

Denpasar Music Festival is a one-day music festival that celebrates the diversity and richness of Indonesian music. The festival features various genres of music, from rock, pop, indie, to traditional music. The festival also showcases the beauty and culture of Bali, the island of gods. Come and join us for an unforgettable musical journey in paradise.
